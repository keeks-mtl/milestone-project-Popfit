Popfit Signature

Get ready to sweat! Grounded in music, our dance fitness Signature class is a full body workout. Easy to follow moves, no equipment. This class is challenging, fat burning, strengthening and super fun! Ideal for weight loss, toning and improving your overall fitness. Suitable for all levels.

Hiit

This is HIIT! This class focuses on getting the heart-rate up through intense bodyweight work and cardio training. The music is high energy for maximum motivation, this is interval training at its best. For those not wanting to dance but wanting to work out whilst still connecting to the vibe of the music.

Popfit Stretch

A vinyasa flow yoga class, using movement led meditation, focusing on your breath, slowing down and connecting to your body. Expect to release tension through stretching sequences with a balance of strength to engage your muscles. Suitable for all levels.

Barre

This is our ballet fitness workout that focuses on strengthening and tightening your core whilst using expansive movements through your upper body. Low impact and set to music to keep you motivated and inspired throughout. No equipment required.
