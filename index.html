POPfit Signature Dance Fitness Class offers you a high energy workout set to music with easy to follow moves. Designed to work your whole body with a combination of cardio, strength training and stretch, you’ll enjoy the workouts as much as you’ll enjoy the post-workout glow.

Other classes include Barre, Ass & Abs, HIIT & Stretch. All levels are welcome - if you are new to POPfit, we encourage you to go at your own pace to begin with. Low impact and modifications are available. Your body, your workout.

POPfit's Founder Steph "I am really passionate about creating workouts that you can enjoy as well as being highly effective. As a dancer I want to enjoy moving my body but I also want to lose weight, tone up and stay really strong and fit. Not only that but I believe in exercise for your mental wellbeing. Moving to music feels good, it's brilliant for your mood".
